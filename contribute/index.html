Contribute to the Initiative

The Agentic Accessibility Initiative and AWGS are designed to be maintained through open governance, public review, consultation, and contributor participation.

Vision work

Help clarify Agentic Accessibility principles, public-interest framing, and explanatory material.

Standard review

Review AWGS domains, compliance maturity targets, conformance language, glossary terms, and exploratory future requirements planning.

Knowledge and research

Contribute knowledge base articles, research notes, future-work proposals, and public review process improvements.

Open Governance

The governance process may include contributors, public review periods, and open consultation. This page does not imply that a formal standards body already exists.
